Independent

Traits to Focus On

  • Loyalty
  • Being aware of limits
  • Doing things one step at a time
  • A feeling of self worth
  • An awareness of people's personal values
  • Patience
  • Being sensitive on one's own and other people's pronounced needs
  • Enjoying the five senses
  • Being content
  • Being aware of being nurtured by Mother Nature.
  • Forgiveness
  • Perseverance

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Being pulled in situation of crises'
  • Being overtly occupied with other people's undertakings
  • Impatience
  • Improper intensity
  • An inclination to be judgemental
  • Getting fixated on others' psychological motives
  • Stubbornly refusing to meet other person (wishes) midway
  • Exaggerated responses
  • To destroy everything in order to destroy only a part of something
  • Obsessive and forcible tendencies

The Achilles Heel of these people is looking for their sense of self worth through others' approval. They need to give up on getting themselves tangled up in other people's doings and focus on themselves, walk on their own path. These people look for a co-dependent relationship, but first, they must understand his/her own needs, to distinquish self, then get into the relationship.
