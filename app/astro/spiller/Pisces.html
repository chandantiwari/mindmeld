No Need For Perfection

Traits to Focus On

  • Being nonjudgemental
  • Compassion
  • Transferring worry to a higher power (flow of life, God, etc)
  • Freeing mind through meditation and introspection
  • Following the spritual way
  • Trusting happy endings, positive results will come
  • Accepting and acknowledging one's connection to Cosmos
  • Welcoming change

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Responses based on excessive worry
  • Overanalyzing
  • Obsessive anxiousness
  • Putting too much emphasis on details
  • Critical first responses
  • Always finding a fault, always trying to prove someone is wrong
  • Worrying too much about making a mistake
  • Being Mr. or Ms. Right
  • Being stuck in uncomfortable situations
  • Being inflexible

The Achilles Heel of these people is their constant need for order and this can push them on an unending quest for perfection of life. But since life never fits any one person's idea of order, they can never feel comfortable enough to let go; they keep postponing their happiness. They need to accept that Cosmos's (or evolving life dynamics') plan is always the right one and better than theirs. When they see this, they also realize the see the bigger picture and as a result let go, be happy.
