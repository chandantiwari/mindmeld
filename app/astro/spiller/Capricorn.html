Responsible One

Traits to Focus On

  • Being in control of self
  • Being an adult
  • Self respect
  • Being goal oriented
  • Solving problems logically, analytically
  • Fullfilling promises
  • Letting go of the past
  • Taking care of self
  • Accepting responsibility in order to succeed

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Limiting oneself due to fears
  • Isolating oneself, spending too much time at home
  • Losing self respect to the point of immobility
  • Using the past as an escape from, or a shield against today
  • Being edgy, whimsical for no apparent reason
  • Being too dependent on other people
  • Lack of self-respect
  • Avoiding personal risk
  • Trying to control others through emotional responses, outbursts

Achilles Heel of these people is dependence. The need of being looked after can force these people in a unending quest for security. They develop attachements, dependence on other people, forcing them to be the center of their security. But this is a bottomless hole. This person must accept responsibility, be willing to take risks and take responsibility for managing and reaching their goals on something. These people long for a sense of belonging but they need to understand a special person or a group will not provide this just because of their need. Instead, they must take responsibility of creating that which they have a need for.
